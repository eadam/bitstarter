What's it about?

It's a series of short films about a cat called Tubs. From the outside he looks like a rotun, fun loving, simple minded creature but on the inside we are witness to his daily philosophical contemplations and obsession with kibble. The films are a comedic look at what life is like for a house cat in the big city.

Why should I care?

If you like comedy, or if you like youtube cat videos (and who doesn't) - these films are for you. The money we raise will go towards the lighting and camera equipment needed to realize my vision of being emersed in the mind of tubs. This is the natural evolution of the cat video, and I hope that the funny, professional shorts can be enjoyed and loved the world over.

FAQ

Where will the movies be based?
The movies will be based in Jersey City, Tub's hometown.
How many shorts are in the mini series?
My intention is to do at least three and measure the response from there. When pledging to this campaign you will receive access to at least three, along with any special extras promised with the amount you donate.
